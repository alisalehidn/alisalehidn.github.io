About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University at Albany, SUNY, where I joined in January 2025. I work with Prof. Daphney-Stavroula Zois in the IMAgINE Lab. My current research focuses on efficient inference for deep learning models and large language models (LLMs), as well as sequential decision making under uncertainty in partially observable environments (POMDPs).

Research Interests

  • Efficient Inference for Deep Learning
  • Large Language Models (LLMs)
  • Sequential Decision Making
  • Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes (POMDPs)
  • Reinforcement Learning
